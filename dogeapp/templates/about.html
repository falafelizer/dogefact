    About

    Here at Doge Fact we’ve taken 30 of the most interesting facts about man’s best friend and translated them to doge for your enjoyment.

    Each subscribed number gets a single text message per day for 30 days. Subscription is a one-time fee of $1, much of which is used to cover the cost of sending all those messages out.

    We give 10¢ of every $1 payment we get to Humane Society International, so you can feel generous while enjoying those facts!
