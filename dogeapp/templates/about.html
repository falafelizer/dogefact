    About

    EvoFacts is subscription based text message service that provides intriguing facts, questions and ideas to ponder for the average consumer. The cost to you is $1. We hope to enlighten people as to why evolution is not merely a “theory”, but rather undoubtedly a fact proven through countless hours of research. Evolution provides a scientific explanation as to the origin of humans by the process of natural selection.

    After subscribing you will receive a text message once per day for 15 days, each daily fact will be limited to two messages and will provide concise information relating to Evolution.

    This project was created by a multicultural team of students holding various beliefs at The University of Texas at Austin.
