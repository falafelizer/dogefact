    About

    EvoFacts is subscription based text message service that provides intriguing facts, questions, and ideas for the average creationist to ponder. The cost to you is $1, which covers the cost of sending the messages. We hope to enlighten people as to why evolution is not merely a “theory”, but rather undoubtedly a fact proven by applying the scientific method across countless hours of research. Evolution provides a scientific explanation as to the origin of humans by the process of natural selection.

    After subscribing you will receive a text message once per day for 15 days. Each fact will be limited to two text messages and will provide concise information regarding Evolution.

    This project was created by a multicultural team of students holding various beliefs at The University of Texas at Austin.
